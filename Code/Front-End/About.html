Our Story

At our store, we are passionate about providing our customers with the best DC superhero models available. Our journey started with a simple idea: to create a space where fans of these iconic characters could find high-quality models to display in their homes or offices. We know that DC superheroes have captured the hearts of fans around the world, and we wanted to give those fans the chance to own a piece of that magic. That's why we carefully curate our collection to include only the best and most popular models, so you can be sure that any model you purchase from us is of the highest quality.

As we've grown and expanded our collection, our commitment to providing top-notch customer service has remained the same. We want you to feel confident and happy with your purchase, so we make sure to provide detailed information about each model on our website and in-store.

Whether you're a die-hard fan of Superman, Batman, Wonder Woman, or any other DC superhero, we're confident that you'll find something you love in our collection. And if you have any questions or need help choosing the perfect model for you, our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always here to help. So why wait? Come visit us at our store on the 8th floor of 379 Hudson St, New York, NY 10018, or give us a call at (+1) 96 716 6879.
